    <TD VALIGN="TOP"> <BLOCKQUOTE> 
        <P ALIGN="CENTER"><FONT FACE="Times" SIZE="5" COLOR="#5D1527"><B>Now You 
          Can Solve Your Workplace Headaches!</B></FONT></P>
        <P ALIGN="center"><font color="#3A5C7F" size="4">I&#8217;ll give you the 
          tools you need to be more successful</font><br>
        </P>
        <p align="justify"> 
          <script language="JavaScript">document.write(specialDateString(new Date()))</script>
        </p>
        <p align="justify">Dear Employees and Managers:</p>
        <p align="justify">Are you frustrated and unhappy at work? Are your team 
          members suffering from low morale and a lack of teamwork? Is your boss 
          is too busy to listen to your concerns and your co-workers too afraid 
          to speak up?</p>
        <p>Surveys show that many workplaces have high stress, low job satisfaction, 
          and high turnover. In fact, job satisfaction has been falling for 9 
          years! Back stabbing, scapegoating and one-upsmanship are also common, 
          along with power games, gripes, and conflicts. You&#8217;ve seen this 
          stuff.</p>
        <p>If you&#8217;re in healthcare, education, government, retail, hotel, 
          restaurant, manufacturing, technology, construction, legal, accounting, 
          or other industry, you&#8217;ll love this book! Employees and managers 
          from these places responded to a survey I did. </p>
        <p>One key finding: 80% of the respondents thought teamwork needed to 
          be increased!</p>
        <p>Is it possible to have a more positive, less stressful workplace with 
          greater teamwork and cooperation from everyone? Yes! </p>
        <p>&nbsp;</p>
        <p align="center"><font color="#5D1527" size="4"><strong>Your Vision</strong></font> 
        </p>
        <p align="justify">Your vision was for a workplace where your boss treated 
          you well, where people worked together to fix problems, and where you 
          could make a difference. </p>
        <p>You envisioned a job where your input was valued, where you were appreciated 
          for a job well done (only 35% of employees are thanked for their hard 
          work). You wanted a place where you had good opportunities for advancement.</p>
        <p>What happened? Your boss didn&#8217;t tell you that he or she was a 
          bossy, unfair person who cared little for your input. Your co-workers 
          didn&#8217;t tell you they were trying to get ahead at your expense.</p>
        <p>Now what? The Teamwork Chronicles, a book that will help you feel empowered 
          again. </p>
        <p align="center">&nbsp;</p>
        <p align="center"><font color="#5D1527" size="4"><strong>Here&#8217;s 
          What I&#8217;ll Give You:</strong></font> </p>
      </BLOCKQUOTE>
      <ol>
        <li>I&#8217;ll explain why some bosses behave badly&#8212;and why it has 
          little to do with you or work issues.<br>
          <br>
        </li>
        <li>I&#8217;ll give you examples of common teams and problems so you can 
          identify where your team falls, in terms of teamwork and performance.<br>
          <br>
        </li>
        <li>I&#8217;ll give you 4 critical teamwork principles that are the building 
          blocks of successful teams and teamwork. You won&#8217;t have to guess 
          about those again! You&#8217;ll know!<br>
          <br>
        </li>
        <li>I&#8217;ll give you reasons why the &#8220;we&#8221; identity is so 
          powerful for real team spirit.<br>
          <br>
        </li>
        <li>I&#8217;ll give you a whole chapter of phrases to help you overcome 
          your fear of raising gripes and concerns. I&#8217;ll also give you the 
          key steps you need to solve gripes and conflicts without confrontation 
          so they can be solved effectively!</li>
      </ol>
      <BLOCKQUOTE> 
        <p>And that&#8217;s just the beginning! I was a very team-oriented manager 
          for many years. Then I returned to the workforce like so many people, 
          and I ended up working in lots of dysfunctional workplaces. It was maddening 
          to see the dumb things that happened! It was also obvious that most 
          people didn&#8217;t know how to do things effectively! Since I&#8217;ve 
          written the book, I&#8217;ve also written lots of articles, had many 
          radio interviews, and been quoted in newspapers and magazines around 
          the country. I&#8217;m trying to make a difference just like you are.<br>
        </p>
        <p align="justify">&nbsp; </p>
        <p align="center"><font color="#5d1527" size="4"><strong>Stories and Anecdotes 
          Galore</strong></font> </p>
      </BLOCKQUOTE>
      <ol>
        <li>I&#8217;ll give you real-world stories so you get the inside scoop 
          on what happens and why. You&#8217;ll understand why things go wrong 
          and to avoid similar problems in the future.<br>
          <br>
        </li>
        <li>I&#8217;ll give you critical insights about managers and bosses&#8212;what 
          makes a good one and how you can be a great manager someday whether 
          you are one now or not.<br>
          <br>
        </li>
        <li>Then I&#8217;ll give you the critical leadership qualities you need 
          to be respected and admired in your workplace whether you&#8217;re an 
          employee or a manager.<br>
          <br>
        </li>
        <li>I&#8217;ll also give you the most important guidelines to follow for 
          you and your team. I&#8217;ll cover other important skills to really 
          make your workplace a great one. Yes, you can make a difference, whether 
          you&#8217;re an employee or a manager!</li>
      </ol>
      <BLOCKQUOTE>
        <p>&nbsp; </p>
        <p align="center"><font color="#5d1527" size="4"><strong>What If?</strong></font> 
        </p>
        <p align="justify">I&#8217;ll give you a chapter of &#8220;What If&#8221; 
          scenarios where I outline common teamwork and team problems. I&#8217;ll 
          give you specific &#8220;Things to Say&#8221; and Things to Do&#8221; 
          so you&#8217;ll have the answers you need. For example, what if a team 
          member is uncooperative, what if a team member tries to dominate the 
          team, what if a boss or manager behaves inappropriately, etc.</p>
        <p>I&#8217;ll also give you great tips and tools to improve your performance 
          on a routine basis. You&#8217;ll see how easy it can be. Watch your 
          job and career improve as you follow these useful tips.
        </p>
        <p align="justify"><br>
        </p>
        <p align="center"><font color="#5d1527" size="4"><strong>Closing Chapter</strong></font> 
        </p>
        <p align="justify">The closing chapter lets you observe a mountain climb 
          with a Peak Performance Team. You&#8217;ll watch this fantastic team 
          climb Blue Sky Peak. Flower-covered meadows, blue skies, fresh air, 
          and the story of a Peak Performance Team will leave you inspired!</p>
        <p>The best part is, you&#8217;ll be able to use the tips and tools from 
          this book in a matter of days. Most people can finish the book in about 
          a week so you can make a difference for yourself and others!<br>
        </p>
        <p align="justify">&nbsp; </p>
        <p align="center"><font color="#5d1527" size="4"><strong>Another Secret</strong></font> 
        </p>
        <p align="justify">As you read this book and absorb the easily presented 
          information, you&#8217;ll be able to use these teamwork approaches and 
          tips in all your relationships&#8212;family, friends, partners, spouses, 
          everyone. You&#8217;ll be amazed at how much of a difference you can 
          make! </p>
