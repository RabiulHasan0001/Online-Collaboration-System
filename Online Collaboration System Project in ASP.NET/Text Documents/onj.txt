<h1 class="green"><b>Online Collaboration Solutions</b></h1>

Organize your work, speed decisions, and enhance your relationships. 
Make collaboration with teams, clients or business partners faster and easier.
Share documents and projects securely over the web.
<br><br><br>
<h1 class="green">Is an extranet or intranet right for you?</h1>
<ul>
<li>Do you share lots of documents with people in multiple locations who need access at 
all times?</li><br><br>
<li>Would a well-organized, shared document archive make your teams work more efficiently?</li><br><br>
<li>Would you like to control who sees what, and when they see it, all within a secure 
environment?</li><br><br>
<li>Does email keep you from sending large documents and graphics?</li>
</ul>
Then an intranet or extranet may be a good solution for on-line collaboration and document sharing. 
But not all solutions are designed the same.
<br><br><br>
<h1 class="green">What makes WorkZone different from other online collaboration solutions?<br></h1>
It's simple. Simple to set up, simple to learn and simple to use...with all the power you need. Other collaboration solutions are crammed full of features that rarely get used and get in the way of effective collaboration.  At WorkZone, we realize that simplicity is a virtue. &nbsp;<a href="javascript:win('/home/tour/client-extranet.vm')"><span style="font-size:8pt"><b>take a quick tour</b></span></a>

<br><br><br>

<img src="/images/client-extranet-advertisers.gif" alt="Client extranet" height=301px width=450px>
<br>
<div class="legend">sample client view</div>
<br><br>
We've simply taken out the complicated stuff that gets in the way of real collaboration. <b>It's simply powerful. </b>
&nbsp;<a href="/home/easy-collaboration.vm" rel="nofollow"><span style="font-size:8pt"><b>more about the WorkZone difference</b></span></a>

<br><br>
WorkZone's elegantly simple, yet powerful extranet design makes online collaboration 
faster and easier. It can be used effectively by computer novices and experts alike, and 
requires very little training. You'll be up and running from day one.
<br><br>
All documents, regardless of size, can be viewed by everyone without concern for restrictions 
and firewalls. 
<br><br>
It organizes everything you share in a simple file and folder system that assures nothing 
is lost, and everything is accessible quickly. You decide who has permission to view 
documents, and all of your work is shared using highly secure 128-bit SSL encryption.
<br><br>
There's no hardware or software to install, and WorkZone requires no IT support. 
It works across any platform - PC or Mac. 
<br><br>
WorkZone was created to help non-technical business professionals use powerful technology 
to collaborate more effectively.
